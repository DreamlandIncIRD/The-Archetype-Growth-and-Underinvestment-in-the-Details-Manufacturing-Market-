# The Archetype "Growth and Underinvestment" in the Details Manufacturing Market

## Abstract
The paper explores the archetype of "Growth and Underinvestment" using the example of a manufacturing company. Three scenarios are considered, with different share of profit erosion in favor of dividends (14%, 7%, and 0%). 
It is shown that any positive erosion eventually leads to collapse: investments decrease, production capacity shrinks, and sales fall to zero. The only sustainable scenario is zero erosion, where the company maintains a balance between investments and dividends, ensuring long-term growth.
